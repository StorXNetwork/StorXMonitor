Navigational Steps to Update Domain-Wide Delegation in Google Admin

To enable auto-sync and allow scheduled mail backups, you must configure Domain-Wide Delegation in your Google Admin account.

Follow the steps below from the Super Admin account connected to your Google Workspace.

Step 1 — Open Google Admin Console

Go to: https://admin.google.com/ and sign in with your Super Admin account.

Step 2 — Navigate to Domain-Wide Delegation

  • From the Admin Console dashboard, go to: Security → API Controls
  • Click on Domain-wide Delegation

Step 3 — Add a New API Client

  • Click Add new
  • Enter the following details:
Field Value
Client ID 104716855463175471781
OAuth Scopes https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.readonly
  • Click Authorize

Step 4 — Verification

Once authorized, the client ID will appear in the Domain-wide Delegation list. This confirms the configuration is complete.

Navigational Steps to Sync and Back Up Corporate Gmail in StorX

Follow these steps to connect your corporate Gmail account and configure scheduled auto-sync backups in StorX.

Step 1 — Log in to StorX

Sign in to your StorX account: https://storx.io/login

Step 2 — Start the Auto-Sync Connection

On the dashboard, locate the Auto-Sync card and click Connect.

Step 3 — Choose the Service

Select Gmail as the service and continue to sign in with your Google account.

Step 4 — Complete Google Authentication

You will be redirected to the Google authentication screen.

  • Click Advanced
  • Click Go to StorX (unsafe)
  • Proceed with the sign-in process

This message appears because the app uses custom domain-wide permissions for backup. It is expected during this setup.

Step 5 — Grant Required Permissions

Allow the requested permissions so StorX can access Gmail data for backup purposes.

Step 6 — Select the Account to Back Up

After authentication, you will see the list of connected accounts.

  • Choose the account you want to back up
  • Click Continue

Step 7 — Configure the Backup Schedule

Select your preferred schedule:

  • Daily
  • Weekly
  • Monthly

Click Proceed to finalize the setup.
